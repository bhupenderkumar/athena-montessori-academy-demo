At Athena Montessori Academy, we believe that every child is a unique and precious gift, deserving of a nurturing and challenging environment that sparks a lifelong love of learning. As the Head of School, I am committed to creating a thriving community of students, parents, and teachers who share this vision.

Our Mission & History

Founded in 2010, Athena Montessori Academy has been serving the Austin community for over a decade, providing a safe and supportive environment for children to explore, create, and grow.

Our Montessori-inspired teaching approach emphasizes hands-on learning, self-directed exploration, and collaborative play, helping children develop a strong foundation in academics, social skills, and character.

In addition to our rigorous academic program, we offer a range of extracurricular activities and clubs that foster creativity, teamwork, and community service, from music and art to sports and outdoor adventures.
