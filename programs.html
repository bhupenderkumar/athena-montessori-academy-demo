Academics & Enrichment

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Toddler and Early Childhood programs (Age 18 months to 3 years) provide a nurturing and stimulating environment for young children to explore and learn through play.

📚

Elementary

Our Elementary program (Age 6 to 12) builds on the foundation established in our early childhood programs, with a focus on academic rigor, social responsibility, and character development.

📖

Middle/Upper

Our Middle School program (Age 11 to 14) prepares students for high school and beyond, with a focus on academic challenge, leadership development, and community engagement.

🔬

STEM

Our STEM program (Age 6 to 12) integrates science, technology, engineering, and math concepts into our curriculum, providing students with a solid foundation in these critical subjects.

🎭

Arts

Our Arts program (Age 6 to 12) offers a range of creative outlets, from music and dance to visual arts and drama, helping students express themselves and develop their imagination.

🏈

Athletics

Our Athletics program (Age 6 to 12) promotes physical activity, teamwork, and sportsmanship, with a focus on developing healthy habits and a love of competition.
