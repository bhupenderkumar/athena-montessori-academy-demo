Why Families Choose Us

Athena Montessori Academy — The Difference

Discover why families across Austin trust us with their children's education.

👩‍🏫

Expert Faculty

Our experienced teachers and staff are dedicated to providing a high-quality education that meets the individual needs of each child. With a strong focus on professional development and ongoing training, we ensure that our teachers are equipped to inspire and support your child's growth and development.

📚

Small Classes

Our class sizes are small and intimate, allowing for personalized attention and a strong sense of community. This enables our teachers to tailor their instruction to meet the unique needs and interests of each child, and to provide a safe and supportive environment that encourages exploration and creativity.

🏠

Safe Campus

Our school is committed to providing a safe and secure environment for all students, teachers, and staff. Our campus is equipped with state-of-the-art security systems, and our teachers and staff are trained in emergency procedures and response.

📊

Comprehensive Curriculum

Our curriculum is designed to meet the individual needs of each child, providing a comprehensive and well-rounded education that prepares students for success in high school, college, and beyond. From math and language arts to science and social studies, our curriculum is tailored to inspire curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking.

🌎

Whole-Child Approach

At Athena Montessori Academy, we believe that education is not just about academics, but also about developing the whole child. Our program includes a range of extracurricular activities and programs designed to promote social, emotional, and physical growth, as well as a strong sense of community and social responsibility.

👥

Supportive Community

Our school is a close-knit community that values collaboration, empathy, and understanding. Our students, teachers, and staff work together to create a positive and supportive environment that inspires creativity, confidence, and a love of learning.

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum that challenges, inspires, and prepares every student.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-Kindergarten program is designed for children aged 2-3, offering a play-based approach to learning that fosters social, emotional, and cognitive development. Our experienced teachers create a nurturing environment that encourages exploration, creativity, and independence.

📚

Elementary

Our Elementary program serves children aged 6-9, providing a comprehensive curriculum that meets the individual needs of each child. Our experienced teachers use a variety of teaching methods to engage students in hands-on learning activities that promote critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving.

📚

Middle/Upper

Our Middle and Upper school programs serve children aged 10-14, offering a challenging and engaging curriculum that prepares students for high school and beyond. Our experienced teachers use a variety of teaching methods to promote critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving, while also fostering a strong sense of community and social responsibility.
